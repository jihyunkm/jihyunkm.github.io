My design philosophy

Before entering the design community, I didn't realize its significance in our daily lives or appreciate good design. However, after working with diverse clients, conducting user interviews, and witnessing my prototypes come to life, I now see design as a tool to understand, support, and empower.

Design is essential to contributing to an empathetic world, and I believe the more designers, the merrier: I've taught over 80 students the basics of Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, an incoming class of consultants the foundations of human-centered design, and mentored 20 student designers.

As a designer, I hope to foster a more inclusive design community and join the hands of those who share the same passion for impact.
