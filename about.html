My design philosophy

Understanding to empathize, then building intuitive & impactful interactions diversity_1

I used to think "good design" meant "pretty." However, this thought shattered the moment I joined a human-centered design consultancy at college.

From solving diverse problem scopes, empathizing with users through interviews, to witnessing the impact of my shipped prototypes, I now recognize that design is a tool to understand, support, and empower.
