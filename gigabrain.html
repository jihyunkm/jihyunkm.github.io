GigaBrain

Mobile Design Design Challenge

overview

GigaBrain is an early-stage start-up dedicated to finding users real answers from real people. During my interview with the company, I was challenged to declutter GigaBrain's search result page within a 4-day timeframe, aiming to present relevant information in a more digestible way.

Using the companies internal insights, I designed and prototyped a high-fidelity prototype of GigaBrain's enhanced search result experience, in an effort to present users with reliable, relevant, and clear information.
